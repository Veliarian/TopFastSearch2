The Best Mid-size Luxury Cars Ranked

  • Automotive

Mercedes-Benz E-Class

There’s no better car in the mid-size luxury segment than this one. A wide array of engines, cutting-edge safety tech, and even a sporty AMG variant makes the E-Class the best.

Audi A7

If you wished the A6 had more style, get the A7. This fastback roof with a hatch opening is the only difference between this and the excellent A6.

Audi A6

The A6 is an incredibly competent car that does everything extremely well. It looks modern and clean and is laden with class-leading technology.

Tesla Model S

This EV offers long ranges per charge, eye-catching style, sporty performance, seating for seven, and luxury that’s a class apart.

Mercedes-Benz CLS-Class

The CLS is for those who need style above all else. This luxury sedan is the E-Class with aggression, power, and style.

BMW 5-Series

The 5-Series dropped its sporty roots and is more luxurious and loaded with more technology than ever before. Of course, it remains capable and very powerful too.

Cadillac CTS

This sedan is more sporty than luxurious. It offers sharp, balanced handling like a BMW, and the 64-hp supercharged V8 is a lot of fun.

Jaguar XF

Handsome style and agile handling are standard with the Jaguar XF. It also offers exclusivity, a lot of engine options, and a menacing engine growl.

Lexus ES

If you want traditional Lexus luxury in new-age styling, this is your car. The ES is a plain, simple, luxury sedan and an excellent overall package.

Genesis G80

If you want a luxury sedan at a bargain price, go for the G80. It’s less expensive than any other car on the list and loaded with all the features you need.

Maserati Ghibli

The Ghibli’s best feature is the sound its twin-turbo V6 makes. Otherwise, it isn’t as luxurious or as well-built as other cars on this list.

Lexus GS

This is a comfortable and composed luxury sports sedan that could only be made by Lexus. It has edgy styling, powerful engines, and a V8 with a fantastic soundtrack.

BMW 6-Series Gran Turismo

It may look strange, but this is an incredibly practical hatchback with more space than a 5-series. It also has all the standard BMW frills.
